About Me

I'm an Android developer, recently graduated from Udacity Nanodegree, with hopes of building up a full stack skillset

My experience is in education and care, working to engage young people with high needs, and stumbled into programming via Scratch and PyGame. In order to keep their attention I had to scaffold their understanding in a creative way, and learn the languages myself quickly enough to give them good answers! My approach to coding flows from that experience - how can it help people? Whether through collaborative platforms, open source assets, or just good design, programming can help enhance the communication and learning people already do. In my spare time I try to learn new concepts and frameworks, help out in the garden on our estate, and perfect the art of pizza.

Latest Projects

Tracktive

An android app to help you engage with the changing world

New

Tracktive is a mobile app designed to encourage engagement with changes in the world. Want to get to grips with world events, on your terms? Tracktive helps you filter stories from hundreds of different news sources, allowing you to track what’s going on, beyond the daily news cycle. Actively pursue and engage with issues. Build cards that search for keywords that you think are important. Bookmark any stories that you think will be important later on. Build a library of evidence and keep a diary of your thoughts.

I wanted to develop an app that encourages us to think about how everyday life is a learning process. The way that we consume news, with its emphasis on rolling coverage, often works against us taking time to think about longer trends, the ‘bigger picture’.
